Dublin, Ireland

Dublin is a very modern and beautiful city, with lots of rich history. There are many important historical and cultural sites throughout the city, with the pubs being most notable among them.

The people are friendly no matter where you go. They all have pretty cool accents. 10/10, would visit again.
